About Us

Welcome to the **Quiz Mock Application**! This web app is designed to help students prepare for their exams by providing them with mock quizzes on various topics. Whether you're preparing for competitive exams, school tests, or simply looking to improve your knowledge, this app offers a convenient platform to test your skills.

Features:

  • Wide range of subjects and topics
  • Timed quizzes to simulate real exam conditions
  • Instant feedback on performance
  • Track progress and improvement over time
  • User-friendly interface for easy navigation
